[h1]Team Ping HUD[/h1]

Adds clear co-op navigation indicators for online and local split-screen multiplayer.

[list]
[*]Cyan edge arrows point toward off-screen teammates.
[*]Yellow arrows and diamond markers show synchronized player pings.
[*]Short press: 60-second ping.
[*]Long press: 5-minute ping.
[*]Ping again near your marker to cancel it.
[*]Native key binding entry under Settings > Key Bindings > General.
[/list]

[b]Default controls:[/b] Middle mouse button / Right-stick click (R3 or RS)

[h1]中文说明[/h1]

为在线联机和本地分屏添加屏幕边缘队友箭头及同步位置标点。

[list]
[*]青色箭头指向屏幕外的队友。
[*]黄色箭头和菱形标记显示玩家标点。
[*]短按保留 60 秒，长按保留 5 分钟。
[*]在已有标点附近再次按下可取消标点。
[*]可在游戏原生按键绑定界面修改按键。
[/list]

[b]默认按键：[/b]鼠标中键 / 右摇杆按下（R3 或 RS）
